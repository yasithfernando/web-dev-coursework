NINE ARCHES BRIDGE

The Nine Arches Bridge in Ella, Sri Lanka is a spectacular example of architectural beauty, renowned for its picturesque charm. Constructed in 1921, the iconic structure spans 91 meters, boasting nine arches, which gave it its name. The bridge is set amidst lush green tea plantations, affording visitors breathtaking views of the surrounding countryside. It is a popular tourist destination, drawing visitors from far and wide who seek to experience the unique beauty of this historic landmark. The Nine Arches Bridge offers a rare insight into Sri Lanka's rich cultural and historical heritage, making it an essential stop for anyone traveling to the country.

KNUCKLES MOUNTAIN RANGE

The Knuckles Mountain Range is a stunning mountain range located in the central highlands of Sri Lanka. This majestic range spans over 155 square kilometers and reaches an elevation of 1,865 meters at its highest point. The area is known for its breathtaking scenery, including lush rainforests, cascading waterfalls, and misty peaks. The region is home to a diverse array of flora and fauna, including several endemic species. The Knuckles Mountain Range is a popular destination for hikers and nature enthusiasts, offering a range of hiking trails that showcase the natural beauty of the area. It is a must-visit destination for anyone seeking to experience the raw beauty of Sri Lanka's wilderness.

BELLWOOD, KANDY

Bellwood Village is a charming colonial-style estate located in the outskirts of Kandy, Sri Lanka. The property spans over 60 acres of lush tropical gardens, including tea plantations, fruit orchards, and a natural forest reserve. The estate features four unique bungalows, each with its own unique charm and style, offering guests a tranquil escape from the hustle and bustle of city life. The estate is surrounded by stunning views of the Knuckles Mountain Range and is a perfect destination for hiking and nature enthusiasts. Bellwood Village is a perfect blend of luxury and tranquility, making it an ideal destination for anyone seeking to unwind in the lap of nature.

HIKKADUWA

Hikkaduwa is a coastal town located on the southwestern coast of Sri Lanka. Known for its stunning beaches and coral reefs, Hikkaduwa is a popular tourist destination. The town offers a range of water sports activities, including surfing, snorkeling, and scuba diving, allowing visitors to explore the colorful underwater world of the Indian Ocean. Hikkaduwa also boasts a lively nightlife, with beachside bars and restaurants serving up delicious seafood and refreshing cocktails. The town is surrounded by lush greenery, making it an ideal destination for nature lovers. Hikkaduwa is a perfect destination for anyone seeking sun, sand, and sea in a laid-back, tropical atmosphere.
